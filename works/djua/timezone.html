In order for the shows to to show up in the correct time at your
location, a few thigs must be known:
1. Your timezone
Your timezone is the most important as it lets us know how many hours you are ahead or behind us. Knowing this allows us to shift the show shedule by the amount of hours you are away from us so that the times you see on the website are your timezone.
2. Whether or not you observe Dalight Saving Time
Some countries observe Daylight Saving Time (DST) and some do not. When adjusting the show shedule, it is important to know whether or your area observe DST.
